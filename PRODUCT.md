# Product

## Register

product

## Users
Both organizers (admin) managing the tournament and attendees (competitors, coaches, spectators) viewing real-time results. Context includes noisy, bright tournament halls, often using mobile devices on the go.

## Product Purpose
To manage and view Kendo tournament brackets and scores efficiently, ensuring operators can run the event smoothly and attendees can easily find their matches and results.

## Brand Personality
Clear, functional, reliable

## Anti-references
- Cluttered, information-dense SaaS dashboards that are hard to read on mobile.
- Overly decorative or "whimsical" interfaces that detract from the seriousness of a martial arts tournament.
- Tiny text and low-contrast elements that fail in bright environments.

## Design Principles
- **Clarity over cleverness**: Information must be instantly readable, even at a glance.
- **Mobile-first**: The primary device for attendees is a smartphone in a busy environment.
- **High reliability**: The interface must inspire trust and never leave the user guessing about the tournament state.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- Standard WCAG AA compliance.
- Focus on high contrast (for noisy/bright tournament halls).
- Mobile-first touch targets and legibility.
