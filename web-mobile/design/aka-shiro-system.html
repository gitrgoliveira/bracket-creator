AKAVSSHIRO

One unmistakable language for telling AKA, the red side from SHIRO, the white side, on every screen, card and row. Differentiated by treatment, not just colour, so it survives glare, projectors, colour-blindness and tiny phone rows alike.

00, THE KEY

Two sides, two treatments

Position is fixed across the whole app: SHIRO is always on the LEFT, AKA is always on the RIGHT. Colour alone is never the only signal, each side also carries its label, its position, and a distinct surface treatment.
